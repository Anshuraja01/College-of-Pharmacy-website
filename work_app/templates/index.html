2.Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm)

The Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm) is an undergraduate academic degree in the field of pharmacy. It is the basic prerequisite for registration to practice as a pharmacist in many countries.The Bachelor of Pharmacy program provides students with a solid foundation in pharmaceutical sciences and prepares them for a wide range of careers in the pharmaceutical and healthcare industries.

Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS)

The Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS) is an undergraduate professional degree in dentistry. It is a foundational qualification for individuals who wish to become dentists and practice dentistry.The BDS program provides students with comprehensive training in dentistry, equipping them with the knowledge and skills necessary to become competent dental professionals and contribute to oral healthcare delivery.

Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) in Nursing

The Bachelor of Science in Nursing (B.Sc. Nursing) is an undergraduate degree program that prepares students for a career in nursing.The B.Sc. Nursing program provides students with a solid foundation in nursing theory and practice, preparing them to deliver high-quality patient care and contribute to the healthcare system as competent and compassionate nurses.

Our Facilities

The facilities available at SHRI MAHESH MOHINI MEMORIAL college of pharmacy are as follows:
Classrooms and Lecture Halls: These are equipped with modern teaching aids such as audio-visual equipment to facilitate effective learning.
Laboratories: Pharmacy colleges typically have various laboratories for pharmaceutical analysis, medicinal chemistry, pharmacology, pharmaceutics, and microbiology. These labs are equipped with instruments and equipment necessary for practical training and research.
Library: A well-stocked library with a wide range of books, journals, and electronic resources related to pharmacy and allied sciences is essential for academic research and study.
Computer Facilities: Access to computers and the internet is crucial for research, data analysis, and staying updated with the latest developments in pharmacy.
Research Facilities: Many colleges of pharmacy have dedicated research facilities where faculty and students can conduct research in pharmaceutical sciences.
Pharmacy Practice Laboratory: Some colleges may have a simulated pharmacy practice laboratory where students can learn about dispensing medications, patient counseling, and other aspects of pharmacy practice in a controlled environment.
Animal House: For institutions involved in pharmacology research, an animal house facility may be available for conducting experiments involving animals.
Pharmacy Museum: Some institutions have a pharmacy museum that showcases the history of pharmacy and pharmaceuticals, which can be an educational resource for students.
Auditorium and Seminar Halls: These are used for hosting seminars, conferences, and other academic events.
Cafeteria and Recreational Facilities: These are important for the overall well-being of students, providing them with a space to relax and socialize.
